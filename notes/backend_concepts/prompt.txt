# Writing Style

- Use proper heading hierarchy (# ## ###).

- Keep explanations concise but long enough to understand.

- Avoid long paragraphs but don't make them one liner either.

- Prefer bullet points. But don't make too short, explain slightly.

- Prefer tables.

- Prefer comparison tables.

- Prefer numbered lists.

- One idea per bullet whenever possible.

- Maximum paragraph length: 3-4 lines.

- Break complex topics into multiple sections.

- Do not write unnecessary filler, but don't make it unclear by removing too much.
